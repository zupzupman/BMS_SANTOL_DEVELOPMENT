Instructions

1. Prepare your Excel file with the following columns in the **first row**, **spelled and cased exactly** as shown:

  • **FIRST NAME** (with a space)
  • **LAST NAME** (with a space)

2. Click **Choose File** or drag the file into the upload area to import your data.
